How to Donate Blood

Donating blood is a straightforward and impactful process. Just follow these simple steps to help save lives and make a meaningful difference:

1

Register and complete a health screening

  • Create a user account on our website
  • Schedule an appointment at a nearby donation center
  • Or register in person at any donation center
  • Complete a brief health screening
  • Undergo basic health checks (temperature, blood pressure, hemoglobin levels)

Online Registration Benefits

Efficient process, convenient scheduling, personalized dashboard

2

Donate your blood

  • Donation process takes 10-15 minutes
  • Sterile needle inserted into a vein (usually in the arm)
  • Relax, listen to music, or chat with staff during donation

What to Expect

Quick and painless process, slight discomfort possible at insertion site

3

Relax and have some snacks post-donation

  • Rest for 10-15 minutes after donation
  • Enjoy provided refreshments (juice, water, snacks)
  • Replenish energy and avoid lightheadedness

Reminder System

We'll send you a reminder every 3 months for your next donation

Before you go, make sure you're eligible to donate by reviewing the eligibility criteria. This will help you determine if you meet the health and safety requirements for donating blood.
