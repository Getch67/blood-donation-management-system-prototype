About Us

At Ethiopia Blood Donation, we're committed to saving lives through efficient blood donation management. Our Web-Based Blood Donation Management System (WBBDMS) addresses the critical need for human blood transfusions in various medical scenarios, including:

  • Surgeries
  • Illnesses
  • Accidents
  • Chronic diseases

By creating a centralized electronic information system for donors and related organizations, we streamline the process of blood donation and distribution, ensuring that life-saving blood reaches those in need when it matters most.
